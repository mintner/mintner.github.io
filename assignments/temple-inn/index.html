Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel that caters specifically to members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints who wish to attend the nearby temple. We have four locations in Bethesda Maryland, Salt Lake City Utah, Oakland California, and Gilbert Arizona. All are welcome, but guests must be willing to abide by our terms and policies. We specialize in meeting the needs of temple patrons, either workers or attendees. The hotel is closed to regualar hotel guests on Sundays. Long-term stay accomodations are available only for full-time temple missionaries.

Washington D.C Temple

❮ ❯

The Washington D.C Temple is currently closed for renovations and will reopen in early 2020.

Construction on the Washington D.C. temple began in 1968 and it was dedicated in 1974 by Spencer W. Kimball.

The Washington D.C Temple was the 16th temple built, and the first on the east coast of the United States.

It was the first to feature six spires since the Salt Lake Temple which inspired the design.
