Our beautiful reception area is designed to sooth and relax you as you prepare to attend the temple.

All temple patrons have access to changing rooms and bathrooms whether or not you are guests of the hotel.

Our temple history themed sitting area is sure to entice you to stay...

The bright, beautiful reception hall can host up to 160 guests and has a food preparation area for outside catering. It is available for wedding receptions and family reunions to make your

special day even more special. Make your reservation today.

We provide low-cost, short-term babysitting for the children of temple attendees. Kids under the age of 12 years old can hang out in our hotel playroom. Playroom hours are Monday through Saturday from noon to 8pm.

Full-time temple missionaries can stay in one of our long-term stay, one bedroom suites with kitchenette. (The hotel is closed on Sundays, so only

emergency services are available to our long-term guests on those days.)

Our business center provides guests access to computers and technical support. You can print from any computer to our air printer while you are connected to the hotel WiFi. A family history consultant will be available to aid guests in family and ordinance searches.

A free contintental breakfast is available to all guests Monday through Saturday from 5am to 8am.

Free WiFi for all inn guests, ask the front desk for login information.

Every room is equipped with a smart TV, and we have a spacious outdoor parking lot to accommodate all our guests-and their guests.

Our discreet maid service is available to all guests--long-term guests for an aditional fee.

Guests can swim year-round in our indoor pool and you won't miss your workout because we have a small gym as well.
